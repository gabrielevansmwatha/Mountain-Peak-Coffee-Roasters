Summit Dark Roast

A bold, intense roast for those who crave a powerful coffee experience.

Origin: Sumatran Highlands

Roast Level: Dark

Tasting Notes: Smoky, Intense, Full-Bodied

The Story Behind the Roast

Our Summit Dark Roast originates from the rich, volcanic landscapes of Sumatra. This intensely roasted coffee offers a bold, robust flavor that stands up to milk and cream, making it perfect for those who love a strong, impactful cup.
