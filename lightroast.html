Alpine Light Roast

A bright, delicate roast that captures the crisp essence of high-altitude coffee farms.

Origin: Ethiopian Highlands

Roast Level: Light

Tasting Notes: Floral, Citrus, Honey

The Story Behind the Roast

Our Alpine Light Roast is sourced from small, family-owned farms in the Ethiopian Highlands. Each bean is carefully selected to ensure the most delicate and nuanced flavor profile. The light roast preserves the coffee's original character, offering a complex and vibrant cup.
